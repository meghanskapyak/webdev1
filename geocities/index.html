About the Site

Hi, I'm Meghan! I'm a web design student, artist, and frog enthusiast. I've been the caretaker for over ten frogs throughout my life, and have learned a lot about frogs as a result! This page is a source for information on frogs, a celebration of frogs, and generally just a place where I can store cute pics and gifs of frogs that I like!

Frog Fun Facts:

  • One gram of the toxin produced by the skin of the golden poison dart frog could kill 100,000 people.
  • A frog completely sheds its skin about once a week. After it pulls off the old, dead skin, the frog usually eats it.
  • A group of birds is called a flock, a group of cattle is called a herd, but a group of frogs is called an army.
  • There is a type of poison dart frog called the blue-jeans frog; it has a red body with blue legs. It is also sometimes called the strawberry dart frog.
  • There is a frog in Indonesia that has no lungs, it breathes entirely through its skin.

A Poem about Frogs

Be kind and tender to the Frog,
And do not call him names,
As 'Slimy skin,' or 'Polly-wog,'
Or likewise 'Ugly James,'
Or 'Gap-a-grin,' or 'Toad-gone-wrong,'
Or 'Bill Bandy-knees':
The Frog is justly sensitive
To epithets like these.

No animal will more repay
A treatment kind and fair;
At least so lonely people say
Who keep a frog (and, by the way,
They are extremely rare).

- The Frog Poem by Hilaire Belloc
