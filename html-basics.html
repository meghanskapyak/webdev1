Mission Statement

Our team at Wacky Wristbands is dedicated to wearable fun! We want you to be the talk of the town, wearing amazing animal and silly shapes up and down your arms.

The Zany History of Wacky Wristbands

Wacky Wristbands was founded in 1986, when our founders Yon Yonson and John Jacob Jingleheimer Schmidt learned how to shape rubber bands using a mold. They experimented with creating simple shapes first, and when those were successful they realized that they had just invented the hottest new fashion accessory! Our sister company, Sillybandz, were founded in 2009.
