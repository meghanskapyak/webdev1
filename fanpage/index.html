Information

Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga and anime series that has been ongoing since 1998. It is written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. The story follows the protagonist, a young boy named Gon Freecss as he attempts to pass the Hunter Exam, becomes a Hunter, learns Nen, and goes through several adventures with his friends. The manga has been adapted into two animes, Nippon Animation's Hunter x Hunter (1999-2001) and Madhouse's Hunter x Hunter(2011-2014). It was also adapted into an original video animation (OVA) from 2002-2004. Hunter x Hunter has been a huge critical and financial success and has become one of Weekly Shounen Jump's best-selling manga series, having over 79 million copies in circulation as of November 2021.
